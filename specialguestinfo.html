    In-Depth Knowledge, Expertise Tipps and Tools From Entrepreneurs and Industry Experts
    Melissa Addey - How To Make The Buyer Call You

    Melissa worked for Sainsbury's Head Office for five years, both as a buyer and as an Innovation Project Manager developing new products and packaging as well as managing the organic range. During her time managing organic food at Sainsbury’s the range won over 20 industry, consumer and media awards including Organic Supermarket of the Year (twice) and went from £170m to £240m turnover.

    For the past six years Melissa has worked on various government business support programmes, supporting small and medium businesses through mentoring, workshops, events, facilitating collaboration with universities as well as consultants and grant-supported projects. She is now working on a book to be published at the end of April. Her talk will empower you to evaluate your product with a purchaser's mind.


    Consulting and Advisory work successes of Melissa include:

    • Café tripled turnover
    • Pastry manufacturer reduced food waste by 200 tonnes
    • Sauce made by a Ghanaian restaurant won Gold Great Taste Award
    • Health drinks and sweetners getting into major retailers
    • Fast-food business developing eco-friendly takeaway containers
