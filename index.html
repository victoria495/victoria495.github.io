About Food Startup School

The Food Startup School is run by the young London-based food events company EventSauce and the well-known Startup & Entrepreneur events and workshops organiser Monkfeet.

We bring together passionnate food and drinks entrepreneurs from London to give attendees the opportunity to gain valuable insights form the successes but, more importantly, failures of fellow food entrepreneurs. Workshops provide concentrated learning, talks offer observations of similar journeys and open for discussion afterwards, and networking (plenty of it) creates the most effective way to bring the industry closer together by encouraging a collaborative environment.
