Hello!

Hi, I'm Victoria, a German girl that loves food & tech.
I came to London in 2014, having previously co-founded and run a Food Festival in Scotland for 3 years. Upon my arrival in the Big Smoke I did some research to seek out the food startup scene and connect with food and food tech entrepreneurs but I quickly realised that the community and events I was hoping for didn't exist. I spoke to about 150 food startups in London over the course of several months and realised that there was a real need and opportunity to create an event and brand that brought entrepreneurs together and would help them take their startup to the next level. You can have a read about my journey here and here.


Making it big

I want to give food startups more opportunities to make it big. We eat so much bad food every single day because it's all that mainstream supermarkets will stock. Interesting products are difficult to discover because many of them don't reach the scale where they could be found in bigger stores. I want empower entrepreneurs with the skills, the network and the inspiration to take their business to the next level.


What's Special About Food StartUp School

With a few incredible entrepreneurs and Food StartUp School supporters by my side, our events bring together passionnate food and drinks entrepreneurs from London to give attendees the opportunity to gain valuable insights form the successes and sometimes also failures of fellow food entrepreneurs. Workshops provide concentrated learning, talks offer observations of similar journeys and open for discussion afterwards, and networking (plenty of it) creates the most effective way to bring the industry closer together by encouraging a collaborative environment. We're working with some incredible international partners and sponsors this year, and I hope you're as excited as I am.
